Overview

b+b Dye Free Ibuprofen is a safe and effective NSAID pain reliever and fever reducer when taken as directed.  We have removed all artificial food dyes and only use quality FDA/USP approved ingredients.  To show kindness to our planet, we have eliminated the excess box packaging used by most OTC brands.

For every b+b product purchased, we will donate a product to a person in need through our national partnership with Americares.  You buy one.  We give one, allowing you to manage your pain while helping others. (maybe provide link to read more about program)

  • Overview
    b+b Acetaminophen works to reduce fever and the intensity of pain signals sent to the brain. It works by selectively inhibiting the production of prostaglandins, a natural chemical released into the body when you’re injured or sick causing nearby nerves to become sensitive to pain and making nearby tissue inflamed and swollen. As a result, b+b Acetaminophen is very effective in reducing fever and pain with minimal side effects.
